Reflection

Both Python and Scratch use functions, conditionals, and for/if loops. In Scratch, you just drag and drop your code, whereas in Python, you need to type it out. I liked Python because it wasn't too difficult to understand. However, the case-sensitivity was a little confusing at first.

Circuit
